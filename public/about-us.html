Our Team

Meelod Waheed

Product Manager

Meelod Waheed is a first-generation Afghan-American at Northern Virginia Community College (NVCC). He is currently studying computer science, concentrating on Machine Learning research and optimization. After NVCC, he hopes to transfer into a top-twenty four-year university to continue pursuing Machine Learning and eventually get into computer vision and creative artificial intelligence. At NVCC, he currently serves as the President of the Phi Theta Kappa chapter, President of the STEAM Club, President of the Cloud Computing Club, and President of the Personal Finance Club. He is also a part of numerous other clubs and organizations at NVCC. He is the Head of Web Development and Design at The Caring Consultants. His role involves designing and maintaining the website with AWS, increasing their online presence, and editing/producing podcast episodes. After graduating, he hopes to become a Machine Learning engineer and start his own artificial intelligence firm.
